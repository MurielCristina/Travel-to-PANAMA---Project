Why Panama?

Panama
Center of the world, heart of the universe.

Legend has it that Panama City was founded by the Spanish conquistadors who saw its potential as a strategic hub for trade and exploration. Much like other historic capitals, Panama City started as a modest settlement along the coast. However, it wasn't until the early 20th century, with the completion of the Panama Canal, that the city truly flourished, becoming known as 'the crossroads of the world'.

Today, remnants of old Panama City can still be discovered. Amidst the modern skyline adorned with gleaming skyscrapers, you can stroll through the charming streets of Casco Viejo. Here, you'll find beautifully preserved colonial architecture, vibrant plazas, and quaint cafes serving local delicacies like ceviche and empanadas. As you explore, you'll see the fusion of history and modernity, with the majestic Pacific Ocean ever present on the horizon.

In every corner of Panama City, the rich tapestry of its past blends seamlessly with the vibrant energy of the present. The city's diverse cultural influences are reflected in its music, festivals, and the warmth of its people. As you wander through its bustling streets, you'll experience a unique blend of old-world charm and contemporary flair. Whether you're marveling at the engineering marvel of the Panama Canal, savoring the flavors of Panamanian cuisine, or enjoying the lively nightlife, Panama City offers a captivating journey through time and tradition, all set against the backdrop of a stunning coastal landscape.

Day Trips from Panama City

DESTINATION HIGHLIGHTS
Taboga Island A quick ferry ride away, known for its beautiful beaches, hiking trails, and quaint village.
El Valle de Antón A picturesque mountain town with hot springs, waterfalls, and a vibrant Sunday market.
Gamboa Rainforest Explore the lush rainforest, take a boat tour on Gatun Lake, and visit the Sloth Sanctuary.
Portobelo Historic town on the Caribbean coast with Spanish colonial ruins and beautiful beaches.

My favorite brunch in Panama

Masi

A chic bistro known for its creative and flavorful brunch menu. Masi offers a variety of options, including eggs Benedict, fluffy pancakes, and freshly baked goods, all made with a modern twist.

Address:

Calle 55 Este, Marbella

What I like about it

Masi's stylish interior and innovative menu make it a popular brunch destination. The attention to detail in both the food and the presentation ensures a delightful dining experience.

Mahalo

A trendy café with a relaxed vibe, known for its healthy and organic brunch options. From avocado toast to fresh smoothies, Mahalo offers a refreshing and wholesome brunch experience.

Address:

Calle 68 Este, San Francisco

What I like about it

Mahalo's focus on fresh and nutritious ingredients makes it a favorite for health-conscious diners. The cozy atmosphere and friendly staff add to the overall enjoyable brunch experience.

Mentiritas Blancas

A cozy coffee shop that serves an excellent brunch menu, including artisan pastries, gourmet sandwiches, and rich, aromatic coffee. It's a perfect spot for a laid-back brunch with friends.

Address:

Av. Central España, Casco Viejo

What I like about it

Mentiritas Blancas combines a warm, welcoming atmosphere with high-quality food and drinks. The coffee is exceptional, and the brunch offerings are both delicious and beautifully presented.
